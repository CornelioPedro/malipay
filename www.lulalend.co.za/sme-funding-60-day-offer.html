How our funding works

Apply online for free.

Apply online in minutes. Tell us how much funding you're looking for and fill in some standard info about you and your business.

Upload your recent bank statements or link your internet banking account to share your read-only transactions data and you're done. Our security is top-notch to keep your info safe.

If you prefer to chat, call us on 087 943 2381 and let's get your application started.

Get a quote.

If you thought the online application was quick, our Lulalend scoring technology quickly evaluates the real-time performance of your business, enabling us to give a quote in minutes.

It also helps us give you the best possible funding for your business.

Once you have your quote, you can choose to accept it and access your funding now or later. There's absolutely no obligation, got it?

Get funded

Our goal is to get funds into your bank account in 24 hours so you can get back to starting, running or growing your business.

Get funded over 3, 6, 9, 12 months or settle early and save with no repayment penalties.

Our costs are transparent and easy to understand so you'll know exactly how much needs to be repaid.
